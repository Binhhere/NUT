## Full Description

NUT is a private, local-first streak tracker for building focus and discipline one day at a time.

Track your current streak, check in daily, reflect before a reset, and keep progress visible without creating an account. Your core progress stays on your device in this v1 release.

What NUT includes:

- Private streak tracking
- Day 1 starts immediately after check-in
- Mindful reset flow with confirmation
- Lifetime clean days, best streak, and reset count
- Milestone badges for 7, 30, 90, and 365 days
- Local demo feed for progress posts
- Optional reminders
- Optional App Lock
- Privacy & Safety disclosure inside Settings
- English, Portuguese, and Japanese localization foundation

NUT is intentionally simple in v1:

- No required login
- No backend account
- No public community posting
- No ads
- No payment flow
- No third-party analytics SDK

Important: NUT is a self-tracking tool for focus and discipline. It is not therapy, medical advice, diagnosis, or crisis support.
