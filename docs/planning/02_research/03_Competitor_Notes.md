# NUT — Competitor Research

## Main competitors

### Brainbuddy
- Platform: iOS + Android
- Price: $12.99/month
- Weaknesses (from 1-star reviews): price too high, too many features, feels clinical
- Our advantage: cheaper, simpler, better UI, community feed

### Fortify
- Platform: iOS only (no Android presence)
- Price: ~$9.99/month
- Weaknesses: not on Android at all
- Our advantage: Android-first, own the space they ignore

### Generic NoFap trackers (5+ apps same name)
- All look like 2018 design
- No community features
- No emotional design
- Our advantage: everything

## Key insight
No competitor on Android has:
- Good design
- Community feed
- Compassionate relapse UX
- Under $5/month

This is the gap NUT fills.
