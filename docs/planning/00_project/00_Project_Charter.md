# NUT — Project Charter

## One-liner
NUT is a dark, calm, achievement-focused self-improvement app built around a NoFap streak tracker — with a companion character and a philosophy of stillness.

## Vision
Most apps fight for your attention. NUT does the opposite. It gives the user a private, quiet space — no notifications screaming, no aggressive gamification, no shame. Just a place to come back to.

## Two versions

### Version 1 — Ship & Learn
A polished NoFap streak tracker. Goal: get real users, real revenue, real feedback. Learn distribution, ASO, and mobile deployment from scratch.

### Version 2 — Companion App
Ryan / Ryana — a manga-style companion character. The app becomes a visual novel light, built around stillness and emotional support. Version 1 funds and informs Version 2.

## Target market
- English-speaking: US, UK, AU
- Android first, iOS later
- Male, 18–30, self-improvement oriented

## Business model
- Free: streak counter, community feed, basic notification
- Premium: $4.99/month or $29.99/year — journal, relapse analytics, advanced stats, home screen widget
- Distribution: organic short-form video (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook)

## Tech stack
- Flutter (Android first)
- Supabase (auth, database, storage)
- RevenueCat (subscription billing)
- Firebase Analytics
